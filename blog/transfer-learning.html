Notes on Transfer Learning

Key Concepts

Fine-tuning: Isolate specific layers for retraining.

Two types of layers:

  • Frozen layers
  • Modifiable layers

Benefits: Saves training time and doesn't require lots of data.

When to Use Transfer Learning

  • Lack of training data
  • Existing network is available
  • Same input for task 1 and task 2

Representation Learning

Finding the best representation of the problem to reduce data with new features.
